# Privacy Policy for Family Chronicle

**Last Updated:** January 31, 2026

## 1. Introduction
"Family Chronicle" ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles your information.

## 2. Information We Collect
We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on our servers.

However, the application allows **you** to input and manage the following types of personal information locally on your device:
* **Names and Biographical Data:** Names, birth dates, death dates, and life stories of family members.
* **Photos:** Images of family members that you import into the application.

## 3. How We Use Information
The information you input is used solely for the purpose of generating your family tree visualization and documents within the application.

## 4. Data Storage and Security
* **Local Storage Only:** All data entered into "Family Chronicle" is stored locally on your personal device (in `.txt`, `.json`, or `.zip` files).
* **No Internet Transmission:** This application functions offline. It does not transmit your family data to the developer, third-party servers, or the cloud.
* **User Control:** You retain full ownership and control of your data. You can delete or export your data at any time by deleting the application files from your device.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository: https://github.com/areteqin/Family_Chronicle
